About Us

Where Students
Touch the Stars

The San Diego Space Institute is a nonprofit organization dedicated to inspiring the next generation of aerospace engineers, scientists, and innovators.

We believe every student deserves the opportunity to reach beyond the classroom. Through our hands-on programs, students work alongside industry mentors to design, build, and launch real CubeSats and small satellites into orbit.

From soldering circuit boards to analyzing flight data, our students experience every phase of a real space mission.

Satellite Design

Students learn systems engineering principles and design CubeSats from the ground up, gaining real aerospace experience.

🔧

Build & Test

Hands-on fabrication and testing in our lab environment, preparing satellites to meet the rigors of space.

🚀

Launch & Analyze

Students witness their work reach orbit and analyze real telemetry data transmitted from their own satellites.
